Larry Kestelman

Director

Larry owns businesses spanning property and construction, international call centres and telecommunications. He has an extraordinary wealth of knowledge and experience in business development so any new venture backed by Oxygen Ventures directly benefits from his acute business acumen. Having recently sold Dodo Australia for over $200 Million, Oxygen Ventures serves as the vehicle to invest in and grow exciting new businesses.

Ilya Frolov

Principal

Ilya is the General Manager and Principal of Oxygen Ventures, his focus is seeking out opportunities and investment pitches as well as coordinating day-to-day operations. His experience consists of more than 15 years working with software development and digital technologies, spanning financial sector, large e-commerce and mobile projects. Ilya's expertise supports every facet of business development, from marketing to human resources, project management to startup strategy.

Boris Rozenvasser

Principal

Whip smart - Boris is a capable businessman with an eye for a proposition and incisive business vision. Former General Manager of Dodo Australia and entrepreneurial business owner, Boris has a keen eye for business models and can spot an opportunity in a heartbeat. That acute perspective is equally capable of discerning problems, but with Boris' ingenuity and experience, there are few that can't be solved..

Tony Mulvany

Head of Design & UX

Tony has been crafting interactive technologies for nearly a decade; and it shines in the rich, engaging and immersive user experiences he defines. Providing strategic, technical and creative direction, Tony is involved in all facets of the design process from concept through to delivery. Managing a team of front-end developers and designers, he ensures the highest standards and best practices are met. Tony has worked in a strategic, creative and technical capacity with a wide variety of clients including: Google, YouTube, Orange (France Telecom), KPMG, Intel and Sony Music.

Ian Pestelos
